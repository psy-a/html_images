Welcome to Art 101

Art is the expression or application of human creative skill and imagination, typically in a visual form such as painting or sculpture,
producing works to be appreciated primarily for their beauty or emotional power.

In simple terms, Art is generally understood as any activity or product done by people with a communicative or aesthetic purpose,
something that expresses an idea, an emotion or, more generally, a world view.
It is a component of culture, reflecting economic and social substrates in its design.

Different people have different views or perspectives towards art. This has led to a never ending debate of what art is good and what art is bad.

You may have seen simple art pieces sell for millions or dollars which to your view shouldn't cost more than a Starbucks coffee. What does this tell us?

This proves the proverbial statement of "beauty lies in the eyes of the Beholder."

Since we have established that everyone has his/her view on which art is good or bad, how do you know your taste for art?

I know mine I love the siple colorful art pieces, either abstract or otherwise.
